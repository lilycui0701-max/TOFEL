Useful Speaking Structure

1. State your opinion clearly

Start with one direct answer so the listener immediately knows your position.

Sentence starter: “I strongly prefer ... because ...”

2. Give your first reason and a short example

Support your opinion with one concrete reason, then add a brief personal or realistic example.

Sentence starter: “My first reason is ... For example, ...”

3. Add a second reason with another example

Keep your second point different from the first so your answer sounds balanced and complete.

Sentence starter: “Another important reason is ... For instance, ...”

4. Finish with a concise conclusion

End with one short final sentence that restates your opinion confidently.

Sentence starter: “For these reasons, I believe ...”
